Welcome to Our Christ

At Friends in Christ, we believe that faith is not just lived on Sundays but every day, in every family, and in every act of kindness.
Together, we celebrate Christ’s love, deepen our spiritual journey, and build a supportive family of believers.

"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." (Matthew 18:20)"
